fix(lua): cap dialogue length to prevent gStringVar4 overflow

BUG: gStringVar4 is 1000 bytes (0x3E8, verified in string_util.c),
but encodeEmerald/writeToBuffer wrote with no length limit. A long
or runaway LLM reply would overflow into adjacent EWRAM and corrupt
other game state.

FIX: cap encoded output at MAX_DIALOGUE_BYTES (250) -- safely under
the buffer and small enough to fit the dialogue box.

Confirmed gStringVar4 is the injection target: AddTextPrinterForMessage
(menu.c) prints from gStringVar4 on window 0.
