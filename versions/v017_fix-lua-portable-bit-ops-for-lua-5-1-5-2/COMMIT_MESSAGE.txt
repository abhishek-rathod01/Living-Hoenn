fix(lua): portable bit-ops for Lua 5.1 / 5.2 / LuaJIT compatibility

BUG: species decode used native ~ (XOR) and & (AND), which only exist
in Lua 5.3+. mGBA's build links system Lua (>= 5.1) or LuaJIT, where
those operators are a LOAD-TIME SYNTAX ERROR -- the entire script
would fail to load, not just the decode.

FIX: replace with arithmetic u32()/bxor() helpers that work on every
Lua version. Masks (& 0xFFFF, & 0xFFFFFFFF) become modulo.

Verified: portable ops equal native across 5000 cases; party reader
still decodes correctly under both signed and unsigned read32.
