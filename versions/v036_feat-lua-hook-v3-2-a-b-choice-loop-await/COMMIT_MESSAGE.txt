feat(lua): hook v3.2 -- A/B choice loop (await_choice action)

New action await_choice:FRAMES opens a window where the hook polls
A/B (emu:getKey, verified in mGBA source) and replies with
{'choice':1|2}; no press -> {'choice':0} on timeout. Powers the TV
quiz and future PokeNav conversations.

BUG CAUGHT BY THE SUITE BEFORE COMMIT: choiceFrames was declared
AFTER runAction, so runAction assigned a global while onFrame read
a never-changing local -- the choice never fired. Worse, the first
diagnostics 'passed' because they instrumented the variable global,
i.e. tested a mutated file. Fixed by declaring the local before its
first writer; both press and timeout paths now verified against the
UNMODIFIED file.
